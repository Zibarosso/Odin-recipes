Potato Salad

Home

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Boil the diced potatoes in salted water until tender, about 10-15 minutes. Drain and let cool.

In a large bowl, mix together the mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, apple cider vinegar, celery, red onion, and dill.

Add the cooled potatoes to the dressing and gently mix until well coated.

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour before serving.
